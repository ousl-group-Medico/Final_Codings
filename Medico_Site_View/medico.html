About Us

Medico healthcare systems,is a global provider of complex solutions in the healthcare sector by connect doctors,patient and pharmacist through one platform.

  • Our Mission
    To provide patient-centered healthcare with excellence through high quality, service and access.
  • Our Vision
    Making your health easier with impressive service

This software application connects doctors, patients, and pharmacists for a better health care service.

  • We create a system that patients, doctors, and pharmacists are connected with each other and there will be no need for paper works.
  • Patients can Sign up with the system with their personal details.
  • Each patient has a unique QR code that shows the patient's history like what kind of disease they had, what kind of medicine they took, and also from where and from which doctor they got medicine.
  • The doctor can easily check the patient and if there are any prescriptions the doctor can add them to the system.
  • The pharmacist will be able to scan the QR and see the medicine and dosage of the medicine the patient wants.
